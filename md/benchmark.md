### Chinese

| Label  | Description  | Example |
|:------------- |:---------------:| :-------------|
| ACOMP     | Adjectival complement | Adjective phrase that modifies the head of a `VP` \| `SINV` \| `SQ`, that is usually a verb. Example: "She looks so beautiful" `so beautiful` is the ACOMP of `looks`  |
